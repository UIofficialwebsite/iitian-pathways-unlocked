## What Requires Google Search Console Action (Cannot Be Done in Code)

### Sitelinks (Page Links Below Main Result)

The sitelinks showing below your main result are **100% automatically generated by Google**. Here's what you need to do in Google Search Console:

1. **Verify Site Ownership**: Ensure your site is verified in Google Search Console
2. **Submit Sitemap**: Go to "Sitemaps" section and submit `https://unknowniitians.com/sitemap.xml`
3. **Request Indexing**: For important pages, use "URL Inspection" and request indexing
4. **Wait for Crawling**: Google needs time to crawl and understand your site structure
5. **Improve Internal Linking**: Make sure all important pages are well-linked from the homepage

Google generates sitelinks based on:
- Site structure and navigation
- Internal linking patterns
- User behavior and click patterns
- Page popularity and relevance

### Logo in Search Results

For the logo to appear:
1. The JSON-LD Organization schema with logo is essential
2. Logo should be a square image (minimum 112x112 pixels, recommended 512x512)
3. Google may take weeks to process and display the logo

### Re-indexing After Changes

After we make these code changes:
1. Go to Google Search Console
2. Use "URL Inspection" for your homepage
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Wait 1-7 days for Google to recrawl
